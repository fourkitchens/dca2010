Discussion: Good practices in Drupal co-development with multiple developers as a group

Bronius Motekaitis | LevelTen room

Have you ever wondered what the best way to co-develop on a Drupal project might be? I know I have..

Let’s have a round-table discussion on what challenges face development shops and contractors working locally or remotely and what tools and methods we can use to overcome those hurdles.
